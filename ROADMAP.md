Roadmap & Prioritization

Phase 1 — Launch MVP (0–3 months)
- Football Reels (upload + highlight detection)
- Basic Player/Club stat cards (cached partner data)
- Live xG timeline (visual only)
- Profiles, Fan Hubs, and basic badges
- Micro predictions + daily leaderboards

Phase 2 — Engagement & Tactics (3–6 months)
- Tactical Stories and Formation Planner
- Heatmaps, pass networks, and live player ratings
- Coach Mode polls and Coach-of-the-Week badge
- AI highlight composer (personalized)

Phase 3 — Immersive & Premium (6–12 months)
- AR replays and multi-angle VAR rewind
- Set-piece simulator and advanced analytics
- Verified player channels and monetization model
- Global reaction heatmap and large-scale watch parties

Prioritization rules
- Impact vs Effort: prioritize features that drive live match retention (live timeline, micro predictions, reels)
- Data dependency: delay features requiring licensed event feeds until partner integrations are secured
- Revenue paths: promote verified player channels and premium replays as monetizable features

Success criteria per phase
- Phase 1: 50k clips processed/month, 20% DAU engage with live timelines
- Phase 2: 40% uplift in session length for engaged fans; coach polls reach 10k votes per match
- Phase 3: paid subscriptions or micro-payments for verified content; AR shares go viral

Stakeholders
- Product: roadmap and feature prioritization
- Engineering: infra and API delivery
- Data/ML: highlight detection, xG, ratings
- Partnerships: stats providers, leagues/clubs
